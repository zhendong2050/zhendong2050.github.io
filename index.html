Bio

Zhen Dong is an Associate Professor at Fudan University, China. Previously, he was a Senior Research Fellow at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore. He received his PhD in Computer Science from Heidelberg University, Germany.

His research focuses on software reliability and security, with an emphasis on mobile computing. He serves on the Board of Distinguished Reviewers of ACM Transactions on Software Engineering and Methodology.
