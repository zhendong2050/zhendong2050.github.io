Bio

Zhen Dong is an Associate Professor at Fudan University, China. Previously, he was a senior research fellow at school of computing science of the National University of Singapore. Zhen completed his PhD in computer science from Heidelberg University in Germany. His research is focused on the development of techniques and tools for improving software reliability and security, particularly in the area of mobile computing. He is currently serving on the Board of Distinguished Reviewers of ACM Transactions on Software Engineering and Methodology. His research won the ACM Distinguished Paper Award at ICSE'20 and the Best Paper Award at AsiaCCS'21.
